HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Controls

Tap a unit to select it.
Tap an adjacent same unit to merge!
Tap an empty cell to move.
3 new units spawn after each move.

💧 Merge Chain

Merge 2 identical units
to evolve to the next tier.

💥 Combo System

Chain merges for combo multiplier!
Higher combos = more points.
Moving resets the combo.

🎁 Special Items

Earn special items by meeting conditions!
Tap the item slot on the board to use.
Each item holds up to 3 max.

Purify — Earned on Crown merge
Tap a unit to remove all of that type from the board

Bomb — Earned every 7 combos
Tap any spot to destroy all units in a 3x3 area

Transform — Earned at 1M points
Tap a unit to upgrade it by one tier
(Cannot be used on max-tier Trident)

Note: Items cannot affect Hall of Fame cells.
Tap the active item again to cancel.

🏆 Hall of Fame

Merge two max-tier units (Trident) to
permanently claim a Hall of Fame cell!

+1,000,000 bonus points
Cannot be removed by any item
More = higher prestige!

⚠️ Game Over

When the board is full and
no merges are possible, game ends!
Place your units strategically.

RANKING
1st: Trophy Count
Players with more Hall of Fame trophies rank higher

2nd: Total Score
If trophy count is equal, higher total score ranks higher
